About Us

Introduction

Welcome to Grit Dominate! I'm Amiel De Los Reyes, your fitness guide with over 13 years of industry experience. My journey began in grade 4 when I discovered my passion for basketball. The discipline, perseverance, and teamwork from those early days laid the foundation for my fitness career.

I'm here to show you that, with unwavering determination and persistent effort, any fitness goal is attainable. Whether you're striving to sculpt your physique, enhance your athletic performance, or improve your overall well-being, I'm committed to helping you reach your full potential. Together, we'll conquer challenges, transform setbacks into comebacks, and turn your aspirations into reality.

Join me on this fitness journey. Let's redefine your limits and reveal a fitter, more confident you. Stay focused, and together, we'll achieve your goals!.
