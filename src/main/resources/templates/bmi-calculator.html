CALCULATE YOUR BMI

BMI, or Body Mass Index, is a numerical measure that assesses an individual's body weight in relation to their height, providing a basic indicator of whether they are underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. It is a widely used tool for assessing general health and fitness.
